Project & Client Introduction


Project Description:
An Augmented Reality Portal on GOSH DRIVE's Azure to take young patients to a virtual operation theatre. Entering a virtual doorway would lead to an operation theatre setting with interactable objects. As well as this, there should be a web application that allows Doctors to customise and select different operation theatres and rooms.

Client:
NTT DATA is a global innovator, delivering technology driven services and solutions to global clients. Headquartered in Tokyo, NTT DATA invests in R&D every year, continously researching technology and society trends that will impact the business in the future.
Great Ormond Street Hospital strives to provide top clinical care for all their patients, based in London.

Project Goals:

The main goal of this project is to help younger patients who may be initially afraid of operations. This app allows them to experience the setting beforehand, so they can familiarise themselves beforehand. This app can then be expanded to model all sorts of rooms and settings for future use, such as for training and so forth.
